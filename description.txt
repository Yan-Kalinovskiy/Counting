-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A. OPTIONS
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  1. Mathematical operations with two numbers (addition, subtraction)
  2. All numbers are natural (from 0 to 9)    
  3. The values of numbers and mathematical symbols are generated in random order
  4. After entering the answer, a message appears about the success of solving the
     expression

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
B. DATA 
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Mathematical expression 
  - Area for entering the answer 
  - Button to send the answer
  - Message about successful or unsuccessful solution
    of the expression (Success! / Error!)
